The Problem

The LSA Lead Problem

Agencies running LSA campaigns face the same bottleneck: manually reviewing every lead is slow, inconsistent, and doesn't scale.

Leads Pile Up Fast

Your team gets dozens of LSA leads daily across multiple client accounts. Reviewing each call recording and message thread takes 3-5 minutes per lead.

Good Leads Get Missed

When lead review falls behind, booked appointments go unconfirmed in Google. Your Quality Score drops. Google sends fewer leads. Revenue falls.

Manual Review Doesn't Scale

Adding more team members to review leads cuts into margins. And human reviewers are inconsistent — the same lead gets different scores depending on who reviews it.

How It Works

Three Steps to Automated Lead Scoring

Go from manual lead review to fully automated scoring and disposition in minutes.

1

Connect Your Google Ads Account

Link your MCC or individual accounts in one click. ScoreLSA automatically discovers all your LSA-enabled sub-accounts.

2

AI Scores Every Lead

Our AI analyzes call recordings, transcripts, messages, and emails — then scores each lead based on intent, urgency, and booking likelihood.

3

Leads Are Auto-Dispositioned

High-scoring leads are marked BOOKED in Google automatically. Low-quality leads are declined. Your Google Quality Score improves, and you get more leads.
